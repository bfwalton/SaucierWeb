Features

Cook smarter, not harder

Saucier is packed with features to make your cooking experience seamless and enjoyable.

Simple Design

Tired of searching for that recipe every time? Our intuitive layout will have you cooking in no time.

Search And Filter

Quickly filter and search your recipes for easy access to exactly what you need.

Folders

Organize your recipes into folders by cuisine, occasion, or any system that works for you.

Save From The Web

Easily save recipes from your favorite websites using the built-in app extension.

Add Notes

Add recipe notes and reminders for next time you cook, so you never forget your adjustments.

Edit Recipes

Needs a little more salt? Easily edit your saved recipes to match your preferences.

Grocery Lists

Easily save your recipe ingredients to your Reminders app grocery list with one tap.

Sharing

Share your recipes with friends! Either send them a sauce file or the recipe URL.

iCloud Sync

Keep all of your recipes backed up and available on all your Apple devices automatically.
